## ❓ **Frequently Asked Questions**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="🔐 How do I sign up for VitalMatrix?">
    You can sign up using either:
    - **Email & Password**: Create a new account with your email
    - **Google Sign-In**: Use your existing Google account for instant access
    
    Both methods give you full access to all VitalMatrix features.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🍎 How does NutriLens work?">
    NutriLens uses AI to analyze food items through:
    - **Barcode Scanning**: Scan product barcodes for instant nutrition info
    - **Photo Analysis**: Take pictures of food for AI-powered analysis
    - **Grok AI Insights**: Get personalized nutrition recommendations
    
    All data is saved to your profile for tracking and insights.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🏥 What is Community Pulse?">
    Community Pulse is our healthcare facility review system that provides:
    - **Real-time Wait Times**: See current wait times at nearby facilities
    - **Community Reviews**: Read and write reviews for healthcare providers
    - **Facility Search**: Find hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies near you
    - **Interactive Maps**: Navigate to facilities with integrated Google Maps
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="📊 How do I view my health insights?">
    Your health insights are available in multiple places:
    - **Dashboard**: Overview of your health and nutrition data
    - **Profile**: Detailed health goals and tracking
    - **NutriLens**: AI-powered nutrition analysis and recommendations
    
    All insights are personalized based on your activity and preferences.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🔒 Is my data secure?">
    Yes! VitalMatrix uses enterprise-grade security:
    - **Firebase Authentication**: Secure user authentication
    - **Encrypted Data**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
    - **Privacy Controls**: You control what data is shared
    - **HIPAA Compliance**: We follow healthcare data protection standards
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="📱 Is VitalMatrix available on mobile?">
    VitalMatrix is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on:
    - **Mobile Browsers**: Full functionality on iOS and Android
    - **Desktop**: Complete experience on all major browsers
    - **Offline Mode**: Core features work without internet connection
    
    You can add it to your home screen for a native app experience.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 🔧 **Troubleshooting**

### **Authentication Issues**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear Browser Cache">
    Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try signing in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email Verification">
    Make sure you've verified your email address if using email/password signup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Google Sign-In">
    If email login isn't working, try using Google Sign-In as an alternative.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If issues persist, email [joe@joepro.ai](mailto:joe@joepro.ai) with details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### **NutriLens Not Working**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Camera Permissions">
    Ensure your browser has permission to access your camera for photo analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Different Lighting">
    For barcode scanning, ensure good lighting and steady hands.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use Alternative Methods">
    If camera isn't working, try uploading a saved photo instead.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Report the Issue">
    Email [joe@joepro.ai](mailto:joe@joepro.ai) with details about what's not working.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### **Community Pulse Location Issues**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Location Services">
    Allow location access in your browser for accurate facility search.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Internet Connection">
    Ensure you have a stable internet connection for map loading.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Manual Search">
    Use the address search if automatic location detection isn't working.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the Page">
    Sometimes a simple page refresh resolves map loading issues.
  </Step>
</Steps>
