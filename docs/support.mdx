## ğŸ”§ **Troubleshooting**

### **Authentication Issues**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear Browser Cache">
    Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try signing in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email Verification">
    Make sure you've verified your email address if using email/password signup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Google Sign-In">
    If email login isn't working, try using Google Sign-In as an alternative.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If issues persist, email [joe@joepro.ai](mailto:joe@joepro.ai) with details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### **NutriLens Not Working**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Camera Permissions">
    Ensure your browser has permission to access your camera for photo analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Different Lighting">
    For barcode scanning, ensure good lighting and steady hands.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use Alternative Methods">
    If camera isn't working, try uploading a saved photo instead.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Report the Issue">
    Email [joe@joepro.ai](mailto:joe@joepro.ai) with details about what's not working.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### **Community Pulse Location Issues**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Location Services">
    Allow location access in your browser for accurate facility search.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Internet Connection">
    Ensure you have a stable internet connection for map loading.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Manual Search">
    Use the address search if automatic location detection isn't working.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the Page">
    Sometimes a simple page refresh resolves map loading issues.
  </Step>
</Steps>
